Talking Parrot

Talking Parrot is a vocabulary learning app, and focused on learning new vocabulary.

My Process

I followed the Design Thinking Process to design the new project. Below you can see the five important phases that structured my design process.

Research

To get to know the existing market of vocabulary learning applications and websites I checked 3 Apps: Learn English Vocabulary, Memerise, and Quizlet.

Ideation

Based on the insights gained from the competitor analysis, user research, and card sorts with potential users, I defined the sitemap for Talking Parrot.  

Usability Test

After several tests I found some usability issues, ranked them and planned the possible next steps for this project.

Prototype

I used Marvel to create a clickable low-fidelity prototype.
